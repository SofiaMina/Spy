Towards the end, she accidently gives away her cover and she is hit on the head by Rayna. She wakes up tied up in a room and infront of her is Fine and Rayna. It turns out that Fine had faked his death. Susan is put in a room with a fellow agent. She feels deppresed and she feels like she should give up. Susan does not listen to Fine and escapes from the building and drives over to where the nuke is being sold. She ends up stopping the nuke from being sold and decided to keep on being a field agent because she realizes she is pretty good at it.
