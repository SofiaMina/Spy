Introduction

Spy was released June 5th, 2015. It is has a rating of 95% on Rotten Tomatoes and made 235.7 million dollars in the box office. The movie follows CIA agent Susan Cooper (potrayed by Melissa Mcarthy) who works in a basement. She is given the oppurtunity to work in the "field" where she gets to directly stop huge crimes instead of just helping the other field workers from her computer. She is given different profiles or identity's and has to work with obnoxious field workers like Rick Ford (potrayed by Jason Statham). She meets new people and has to go undercover to work with criminals. The plot is funny and exciting.

Plot summary

Susan Cooper ends up stuck in a sticky situation. She assists CIA field agent Bradly Fine from the basement office. Fine enter the home of a rich women named Rayna who is trying to buy a nuke to sell it for millions and millions of dollars. Susan witnesses the murder of Fine when Rayna shoots him. Rayna knows someone his watching her and reveals private information about the agency and all the names of the active field agents at the time. Clearly there was a mole who gave Rayna the information that FIne was going to be there. Since Susan is very unknown to all criminals because she works in the basement they decide to send her because of how much she wants to avenge her friend, coworker and crush Fine. She goes to the field and uncovers information about the buyer of the nuke. The buyer, De Luca is a rich business who is known to be linked to terrorists. She figures out that they will be meeting in Rome and she figures out that Rayna and De Luca are going to be at a famous casino and she decided to go and see if she can collect intell. She ends up meeting a coworker, Rick Ford, who insults her and walks away because he wants to stop De Luca and Rayna himself. At the casino, she spots Rayna and she see's someone poison her drink. She decides to let Rayna know while she trys to control her anger. Rayna thanks her and offers to take her to dinner. Now Susan need to keep herself undercover so she can get closer to her. Towards the end, she accidently gives away her cover and she is hit on the head by Rayna. She wakes up tied up in a room and infront of her is Fine and Rayna. It turns out that Fine had faked his death. Susan is put in a room with a fellow agent. She feels deppresed and she feels like she should give up. Fine then walks in and says that he faked his death so Rayna can trust him. He explains that he is also trying to stop the nuke from being sold and tells her to wait in the room. Susan does not listen to Fine and escapes from the building and drives over to where the nuke is being sold. She ends up stopping the nuke from being sold and decided to keep on being a field agent because she realizes she is pretty good at it.

Why I liked the movie

When I first watched this movie I thought it was really funny. I always liked the spy and undercover type of plot. This had that exciting mystery type plot but with a lot of comedy. I almost couldn't go five minutes without laughing. The ways the characters interacted made everything funnier. Although some people call it a rip-off James Bond because it had to much profanity and was not as realistic, but I disagree. It was just as interesting and is meant to comedy so I guess the profanity was kinda needed. I highly reccomend this movie.
